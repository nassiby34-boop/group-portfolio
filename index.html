Welcome!

Welcome to our Student Portfolio Website. This website was created as a group project by first-year students at the University of Dar es Salaam, Tanzania. Here you will find personal portfolio pages for each member of our group, showcasing our backgrounds, skills, education, and interests.

Use the navigation menu above to explore each student's personal page, or visit our Contact page to get in touch with us.

About Our Group

We are a group of 4 first-year Computer Science students from the University of Dar es Salaam. We come from different backgrounds but share a common passion for technology, learning, and growth. This project brought us together to collaborate and showcase who we are as individuals and as a team.
