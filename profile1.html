Personal Information

My name is Nasib Yasin Said and I am a first-year student at the University of Dar es Salaam, Tanzania. I was born and raised in Kawe, Dar es Salaam, a city that has shaped my passion for creativity and technology.

I am a creative individual with a strong interest in both the technical and artistic sides of the world. I enjoy writing stories, designing characters, and building things — whether that is through code, drawing, or 3D modelling. My goal is to combine my programming skills with my storytelling passion to one day create my own video games.
